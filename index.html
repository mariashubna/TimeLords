Doctor Who

  • Real Name:

    Theophilus Quill

  • Age:

    Timeless (appears 45 in Earth years)

  • Occupation:

    Time Lord, Guardian of Time and Space

  • Interests:

    Quantum Mechanics, Intergalactic Diplomacy, Ancient Civilizations

  • Needs:

    With each journey to different planets and time periods, Doctor Who interacts with countless beings, each with their own unique identities, backgrounds, and relationships. Additionally, the fluid nature of time and space means that Doctor Who often revisits locations and encounters individuals at different points in their timelines, further complicating the task of mentally managing his network of contacts. Therefore, a convenient mobile assistant capable of cataloging and organizing this wealth of information becomes essential for Doctor Who to navigate his adventures effectively.
